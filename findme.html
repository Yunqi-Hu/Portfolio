Find Me

A mobile social application for foreigners

About the Application

Find Me is a mobile application to connect foreigners with their local fellow countryman. Wherever users are, they can find and be found by their nearby fellow countryman using the same application, hence to connect with them.


My Role

- Product Designer
- UX Researcher
- UX/UI Designer

Design Problem

For a foreigner, it can be difficult to start life in a new country with little knowledge about the local area and language barrier. In situation like this, we normally seek help from people with more common ground of culture and language, our fellow countryman.
However, currently, there is no tool can fully support this need of connecting foreigners with their fellow countryman, which makes foreigners' socialization with their fellow countryman very hard to conduct.

Solution

To address the problem, I propose a location-based mobile social application for foreigners to find their local fellow countryman.
By utilizing the GPS on users' mobile phones and their nationality information from their profiles, users from the same country in local area will be able to know the existence of each other and socialize.

Design Process

User Interviews & Contextual Inquiries

To have a more comprehensive and subjective understanding about this problem, I interviewed 12 international students and conducted contextual inquiries with 3 potential users.
By doing so, some research findings have been made:
- Foreigners would prefer to communicate with their fellow countryman in an instant way.
- Foreigners would expect to hold activities with their countryman.
- Foreigners are willing to share their resources with their countryman.
- Foreigners hope to keep a long term relationship with their countryman.

Persona

Based on the data from user interviews and contextual inquiries, a persona of the typical user of this application has been made.

Ideation & Paper Prototype

I created 40 alternative sketches based on brainstorming result, clustered these alternatives into a few high-level functional categories, and then decided which way to go.
After several rounds of further talk with users on ideation results, the design approach has been finally decided. Based on which, I made my paper prototype.

Features

Feature 1 - Find People

Users can search for their nearby fellow countryman and add them as friends.

Feature 2 - Instant Message

Users can send instant messages to the people in their friend list.

Feature 3 - Host and Join Activities

Users can host activities and post them for their nearby fellow countryman to join.
